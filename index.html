Job Market Paper
Tax Enforcement and Short-Term Rental Hosts: Evidence from Florida
Working Paper, 2025
This paper examines how enforcement shocks affect property tax compliance among short-term rental hosts, using Airbnb Voluntary Collection Agreements in Florida as a quasi-natural experiment. I apply a bunching estimator to AirDNA data (2015–2024) to identify behavioral responses around filing thresholds.
